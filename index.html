Cafe La Reine Employee Competencies

Definitions of required station specific knowledge and abilities for each Cafe La Reine employee.

Cafe La Reine stations of activity include:

  • General
  • Dining Room
  • Cashier
  • Espresso
  • Line
  • Food Prep
  • Opening
  • Closing
  • Midday

General Requirements

  • Understands flow of service (customer ordering experience, how cafe runs, and when to do what)
  • Constistantly does required periodic tasks (checking dining room, doing dishes, and tidy work)
  • Unobtrusively assists coworkers where required
  • knows when to ask for assistance and when not to based on his or her work load and the workload of other staff

Dining Room

  • Mindful whenever out front of when last checked
  • When checking brings sanitizer and rag and checks trashes, tables, litter, coffee bar, coffees, mindful of other less prodictable concerns
  • Keeps out of the way of customers to the greatest possible degree

Cashier

  • Understands layout of POS system and quickly navigates it
  • Friendly with customers, and graciously handles any problems that arrise during customer interaction
  • Balances taking orders with assistance to potentially overrun coworks

Espresso

  • Knowledgeable of all coffee offerings (iced coffees, macchiato, etc)
  • follows procedure for pulling proper shots
  • steams milk properly for variety of milk (basic guide here and a more deep understanding of the fundementals of the process here)
  • keeps machine and surrounding area tidy and clean
  • organizes tasks to effiecently utilize time

Line

  • knows the builds of the various menu items
  • stocks items from below during the course of making food
  • replaces items after use
  • keeps area clean
  • appropriately plates or packages food
  • keeps acai station free of food scraps
  • keeps blenders clean

Food Prep

  • top stocks food units before creating list
  • builds list completely as defined by Eric's complete prep guide
  • dates all prepped items
  • fills food to line in pan
  • orgizes food in a manner to easily facilitate FIFO food utilization
  • slices items on the correct setting
  • makes cream cheeses with proper ingredients and with the proper amount of cream completely whipped
  • pesto is made with basil leaves (no stem), very minimal basil waste, and pitcher scraped clean (again little waste)

Opening

  • understands, has done, and is proficient at all tasks involved in opening

Closing

  • understands, has done, and is proficient at all tasks involved in closing
  • keeps on course with tasks through the night instead of waiting beyond time specified by closing sheet.
  • double checks doors are locked

Midday

  • understands, has done, and is proficient at all tasks involved in midday
